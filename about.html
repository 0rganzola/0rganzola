ABOUT
I'm Drew Shapiro, and I make weird and wonderful games. My work has been featured at a variety of conventions and venues, including GDC, MAGFest, Wonderville, Hex House, and Game-A-Palooza. After graduating from Pratt Institute with a BFA in Game Design, I'm currently working as the sole developer on MUTAGEN as part of the NYU Game Center Incubator.
